Labyrinth game guide

Basic things

After you start the game and you are on the map you can move with the 'w','a','s','d' keys or the ↑ , ← , ↓ , → keys on your keyboard.

  • Press 'w'/↑ to move up.
  • Press 'a'/← to move left.
  • Press 's'/→ to move down.
  • Press 'd'/↓ to move right.

Treasure rooms

There are treasure rooms on the map. You can acquire the treasure if you just need to enter to the room.

Exits

On the picture we show you an example for an exit in the game. If you go out there the game will ask you if you really want to leave the map.

How to save and load a map

Press the 'Esc' key on your keyboard and you will see a menu.

You can navigate beetween the menu points by pressing 'w'/↑ and 's'/↓ , to choose from them press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.

If you want to save choose the option 'Save' the program will ask you to enter a name for the map.

To load a saved map you need to press the 'l' key on your keyboard while you are on the main menu.
